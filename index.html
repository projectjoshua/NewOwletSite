Clinical Proven Technology

Using clinically proven technology called Pulse Oximetry, Owlet tracks a child's heart rate and oxygen level and alerts if levels are higher or lower than preset levels.

HEART RATE

Owlet tracks ou baby's heart rate and alerts you if it falls beneath 60 beats/min or rises above 220 beats/min, the range considered normal for babies.

OXYGEN LEVEL

Owlet tracks your baby's oxygen level and alerts you if it drops beneath 80% of the oxygen amounts considered normal for babies.
